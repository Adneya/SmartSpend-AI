Connect to DroidCam

Use your mobile phone as a wireless camera

📱 Setup Instructions:

  1. Download DroidCam app from Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS)
  2. Install and open the DroidCam app on your phone
  3. Enable WiFi on your phone and computer - they must be on the SAME network
  4. In DroidCam app, tap "Start Server" or "Start WiFi Server"
  5. You'll see your phone's IP address displayed in the app (like 192.168.x.x or 10.x.x.x)
  6. Enter that IP address above and click "🔗 Connect to DroidCam"
  7. Live video should appear below

⚙️ Troubleshooting:

  • ❌ "Connection Failed"? → Check if DroidCam server is actually running on phone
  • ❌ "Wrong IP?" → Copy the exact IP from DroidCam app (not your WiFi router IP)
  • ❌ "Still won't connect?" → Both devices must be on same WiFi network
  • ❌ "Network error?" → Check phone's WiFi is connected, not mobile data
  • 📌 Default port: 4747 (usually correct, change only if DroidCam shows different port)
